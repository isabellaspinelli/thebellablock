Project One Process

I first started by creating a collage of pictures on Pintrest.

I wanted this to be the base of my design.

Next, I went into Instagram and experimented with a few of their editing features on the stories function.

I added the heart in the center with the spotify logo as well.

I then layerd several effects on top of each other.

For example, I added an "Old Camera" effect and a "Glitch" effect.

I then combined my four favourite edits in a grid and continued to add effects on that.

Finally, I added a glitch effect to the text and put them together in Instagram.
